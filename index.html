About Me

Hello, my name is Matthew Williams and I am a junior at the University of New Haven, majoring in Computer Science with a concentration in Cybersecurity. University of New Haven is designated as a National Center of Academic Excellence in Cyber Operations (CAE-CO) by the National Security Agency, providing me with a curriculum centered around real-world application and national standards.

My academic journey has included hands-on, lab-based courses in digital forensics, programming, and networking, where I’ve conducted forensic analysis on simulated cyber cases and sharpened my technical and investigative skills. I have found that I enjoy working with cloud computing, including setting up resources and securing them with Millennium Tech USA.

Outside the classroom, I am employed at my local police department affiliated with my university, which has given me insight into public service and security operations. I am also an Eagle Scout that has worked alongside the American Legion, VFW, local parks, and my hometown First Aid Squad on a number of volunteering projects.
